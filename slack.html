About

  • Slack is a proprietary business communication platform developed by Slack Technologies. It launched in August 2013
  • Slacks features include chat rooms/channels organized by topic, private groups, and direct messaging. Slack is available in eight languages.
  • Slack has public channelsthat allow team members to communicate without the use of email or group texting. Public channels are open to everyone in the workspace. Private channels allow for private conversation between smaller sub-groups. These private channels can be used to organize large teams.

Educational Use -Students/Teachers

Educational use of Slack for both students and teachers

  • Slack can be a very useful social media tool for both third level students and educators, especially during times like this when students and educators can’t communicate in person. The main use for Slack would be to message multiple students in a workspace or individually in direct message. The app also allows people to send files, images and voice nots to others. This makes it super quick and easy for both students and educators to communicate. During my first year of college I have used Slack to create during the “intro to digital media module” we would pick a workspace based on what topic interested us the most and would then be in a group with the others in the workspace instead of creating groups with your friends. This was a great use of the app.
